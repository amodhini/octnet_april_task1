REGION

JAPAN The cherry blossom is considered the national flower of Japan, so it had to bag the first spot. From Mount Yoshino (Nara) to Philosopher's Path (Kyoto) and Shinjuku Gyoen (Tokyo), many places offer picturesque views of sakura in Japan.You can also visit Osaka, Hiroshima, and Hokkaido. While the blooming of the flowers depends on region to region, the cherry blossom season begins by the end of March and goes on till April.
INDIA Head to Jammu and Kashmir to experience an enchanting view of the cherry blossoms. Gulmarg, Srinagar, Pahalgam Baramulla, and Shopian are some regions to find these flowers during March and April.Cherry blossoms also bloom in Mashobra village, which is 10 km away from Shimla.
USA Cherry blossoms bloom every year in Washington DC. Back in 1912, around 3,000 trees were presented by Japan, and the tradition of cherry blossom festivals in the US can be traced back to 1935. Mid-March to mid-April is when you can see sakura here. Apart from Washington DC, New York is where you can see cherry blossoms. Most of NYC's cherry trees bloom by mid-April.
FRANCE Sakura can be found in certain regions of France. One such location where cherry blossoms can be enjoyed is the Parc de Sceaux, located in Sceaux, a suburb of Paris. Head over to the French capital in late March or early April.

ABOUT

Somei yoshino

Somei yoshino (Yoshino cherry) are the most common type of sakura in Japan, and indeed are what many people think of right away when they hear the word ‘sakura’. Blooming before their green leaves even appear, these five-petaled cherry blossoms are almost white, having only the vaguest hint of pink to them. The name somei yoshino comes from a village market that existed during the late Edo era, where it is said that these flowers were first sold.

Fugenzo

The Fugenzo is a late blooming yaezakura with about 30-40 petals per blossom. White to slightly pink when opening, the blossoms turn into a darker pink over time. The fresh leaves are coppery brown.In the case of early blooming trees, the fresh leaves usually do not appear until after full bloom, which gives the trees an attractive, homogeneous look while they are in full bloom. In case of later blooming trees, the leaves usually appear before the blossoms, giving the trees a more heterogeneous look

Hanagasumi

Hanagasumi (flower haze) occurs when many cherry blossom trees bloom in a row, creating a flower haze, or blurry vision, with its pink and white petals. This vocabulary workshop term describes the delicate white cherry blossoms. As these trees often cling to the riverbanks in the early morning, these white petals look like mist across the horizon

Kanhizakura

Kanhizakura flowers bloom on Honshu island in the early weeks of March, whereas in Japan’s southern area, the blooming time usually begins between January and February. Mount Yaedak in Okinawa is one of the beautiful places worth experiencing in February, also the peak season of Kanhizakura bloom.This cherry blossom is originally from Taiwan, yet it’s also commonly grown in Okinawa, Japan. The graceful and exotic of these cherry blossoms come from their bell-like shape and lucid pink color
